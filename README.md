## üìå **Overview**
This project was completed as part of the **Business Analyst Internship Assignment** for **ElectricPe**, a leading EV mobility platform.  
The objective was to analyze the **end-to-end customer journey** from **Lead ‚Üí Pre-booking ‚Üí Booking ‚Üí Delivery** and identify operational bottlenecks, inefficiencies, and actionable insights to improve **conversion rate, delivery turnaround time (TAT), and customer satisfaction**.

---

## üéØ **Objectives**
- Map the complete **lead-to-delivery funnel** and calculate **conversion rates** at each stage.  
- Analyze **store-wise and lead-type performance** to identify high-performing segments.  
- Measure **TAT** (Pre-booking, Booking, Delivery) to locate process delays.  
- Examine **cancellation trends**, **NPS (Net Promoter Score)**, and **customer complaints** to assess satisfaction.  
- Provide **data-backed recommendations** to improve **conversion, reduce cancellations,** and **enhance delivery efficiency**.

- **Lead Types:** Hot (30%), Warm (51%), Cold (19%)  
- **Metrics:** Conversion %, TAT (days), Cancellations (%), NPS, Complaints

| **Area** | **Insight** | **Impact** |

## ‚öôÔ∏è **Methodology**
1. **Data Cleaning & Preparation:**  
   - Processed lead, booking, and delivery data in Excel using formulas and pivot tables.

   - Calculated **store-level and lead-type conversion rates**.

   - Built an **interactive Excel dashboard** with conversion funnel, TAT performance, NPS, and cancellations.  
   - Enabled easy **store-level comparison** and actionable visualization for management.  

4. **Insight Storytelling:**

## üí° **Recommendations**
- **Automate follow-ups** for cold and inactive leads to boost engagement.  
- **Optimize delivery logistics** and track **stage-wise TAT** at the store level.  
- **Introduce flexible financing options** and improve **price transparency** to reduce cancellations.  
- **Launch loyalty and recovery programs** to convert neutral customers (ratings 4‚Äì7) into promoters.  
- **Align service and sales teams** post-sale to reduce complaint spikes.
